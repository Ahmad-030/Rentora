Section 01

Overview

Rentora is a professional car rental management platform for small and medium-sized rental businesses. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Our promise: We collect only what is necessary to run Rentora. We will never sell, rent, or monetise your data. Your business information belongs to you.

By registering a business account or using Rentora as an agent, you agree to the practices described here.

Section 04

Storage & Security

We use enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure to protect your data at every layer.

  • Google Firebase — AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit for all structured data.
  • Cloudinary — Vehicle photos stored on Cloudinary's secure CDN with access-controlled URLs.
  • Passwords — Never stored in plain text. Firebase Auth handles secure hashing and token management.
  • Data isolation — Each company's data is logically isolated with a unique ID. Security rules prevent cross-company access entirely.
  • No sensitive local caching — Booking records and CNIC data are not stored on your device.
Security Incident Response

In the event of a breach affecting your account, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of becoming aware of it.

Section 05

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or share your data for advertising. The only third parties that may process your data are infrastructure providers essential to running Rentora.

  • Google Firebase — database, authentication, cloud storage.
  • Cloudinary — image hosting and delivery.
  • Legal obligation — we may disclose data only if required by law or a valid court order.
No analytics platforms, ad networks, or data brokers have access to your Rentora data. Period.

Section 07

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide the service.

  • Active accounts: Data retained for the lifetime of your business account.
  • Account deletion: All data permanently removed within 30 days of a written deletion request.
  • Agent removal: Firestore profile deleted immediately when removed by an admin.
  • Backups: Firebase automated backups may retain deleted data for up to 30 days before purging.

Section 09

Cookies & Local Storage

Rentora is a native mobile app and does not use cookies. Only these lightweight mechanisms are used for app functionality:

  • SharedPreferences — stores only onboarding completion and permission status. No personal data.
  • Firebase Auth tokens — secure session tokens handled by Firebase SDK in your device's secure storage.
  • No advertising IDs — we do not access GAID, IDFA, or any device advertising identifier.
Section 10

Children's Privacy

Rentora is intended exclusively for adults operating or working within a vehicle rental business. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at privacy@rentora.app and we'll delete it promptly.
